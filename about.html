My Story

From Student to Educator & Author

I'm Castro Odhiambo, a passionate educator and fourth-year student at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Education Science, specializing in Mathematics and Computer Studies.

My journey in education began with a deep curiosity about how technology can transform learning. This passion led me to author two publications: "Digital Competency" and "Practical Guide to Computer Applications", aimed at equipping learners and educators with essential digital skills.

Through CastroHub, I'm creating a platform that combines my expertise in education, technology, and research to make learning more accessible, engaging, and impactful for everyone.
