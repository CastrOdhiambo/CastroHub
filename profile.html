Professional Summary

Educator, Author & Tech Innovator

I am a passionate educator and fourth-year student at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Education Science, specializing in Mathematics and Computer Studies. My academic journey is driven by a deep commitment to bridging the gap between education and technology.

As a published author, I've written "Digital Competency" and "Practical Guide to Computer Applications" to help learners develop essential digital skills. My expertise spans mathematics education, computer studies, digital competency, web development, graphic design, and educational technology.

Through CastroHub, I'm creating an innovative platform that combines educational resources, AI-powered learning tools, and community engagement to transform how people learn and grow.
