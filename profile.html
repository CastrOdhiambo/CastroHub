Who I Am

Passionate Educator & Tech Innovator

I'm Castro Odhiambo, a passionate educator and fourth-year student at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Education Science, specializing in Mathematics and Computer Studies.

My journey in education is driven by a deep commitment to bridging the gap between technology and learning. I've authored two publications: "Digital Competency" and "Practical Guide to Computer Applications", aimed at empowering learners with essential digital skills.

Through CastroHub, I'm creating a platform that combines my expertise in education, technology, and research to make learning more accessible and engaging for everyone.
